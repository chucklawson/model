# Historical Dividends

Track dividend income and dividend history for your portfolio.

## Overview

The Historical Dividends page helps you:
- Track dividend payments over time
- Monitor dividend growth
- Calculate dividend income
- Analyze dividend-paying positions

## Features

### Dividend History

View historical dividend payments:
- Payment dates
- Dividend amounts
- Ex-dividend dates
- Dividend frequency

### Dividend Analysis

Analyze your dividend income:
- Total annual dividend income
- Dividend growth rates
- Yield on cost
- Dividend consistency

### Tracking Dividend Lots

When adding positions on the Tickers page:
- Mark dividend reinvestment lots with "Is Dividend" checkbox
- Separate tracking of dividend shares vs original purchases
- Accurate cost basis calculations

## How to Use

1. Navigate to the Dividends page (Historical Dividend Entries)
2. View dividend history for your holdings
3. Filter by ticker or date range
4. Track total dividend income

## Tips for Dividend Investors

### Building a Dividend Portfolio

- Focus on dividend growth stocks
- Track dividend yield and growth rate
- Monitor payout ratios for sustainability
- Diversify across sectors

### Reinvestment Tracking

- Mark dividend reinvestments in Tickers page
- Keep separate lots for tax purposes
- Track total shares accumulated through DRIP

### Tax Considerations

- Export dividend data for tax reporting
- Track qualified vs non-qualified dividends
- Keep records of dividend payments
