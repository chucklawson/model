# Research Tools

Explore the research features for staying informed about your investments and the market.

## Overview

The Research page provides powerful tools for market research and staying informed:
- Stock-specific news search
- General market news
- Treasury yield comparisons

## Features

### Stock News Search

Search for news about specific companies:
1. Navigate to the Research page
2. Select "Stock News" tab
3. Enter a stock ticker
4. View recent news articles related to that company

### Market News

Get general market updates and trends:
1. Select "Market News" tab
2. View recent market news
3. Stay informed about broader market movements

### Treasury Yields

Compare current treasury rates:
1. Select "Treasury Yields" tab
2. View yield curves for different maturities
3. Compare rates over time

## Tips

- Use stock news before making investment decisions
- Monitor treasury yields to understand interest rate environment
- Check market news daily to stay informed
