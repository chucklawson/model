# Calculators

Financial calculators to help evaluate investments and make informed decisions.

## Overview

The Calculators page provides three powerful financial tools:
- **P/E Growth Calculator** - Evaluate stock valuations
- **Rule of 72** - Calculate investment doubling time
- **Mortgage Calculator** - Plan home purchases

## P/E Growth Calculator

Evaluate whether a stock is fairly valued based on its P/E ratio and expected growth rate.

### How to Use

1. Navigate to Calculators page
2. Select "P/E Growth Calculator"
3. Enter:
   - Current P/E ratio
   - Expected 5-year growth rate (%)
   - Expected dividend yield (%)
4. View the calculated fair value

### Interpretation

- **Result < 1:** Stock may be overvalued
- **Result = 1:** Stock is fairly valued
- **Result > 1:** Stock may be undervalued

## Rule of 72 Calculator

Quickly estimate how long it will take your investment to double.

### How to Use

1. Select "Rule of 72"
2. Enter expected annual return (%)
3. View years to double

### Formula

Years to Double = 72 ÷ Annual Return (%)

**Example:** 8% return → 72 ÷ 8 = 9 years to double

## Mortgage Calculator

Plan home purchases with comprehensive mortgage calculations.

### How to Use

1. Select "Mortgage Calculator"
2. Enter:
   - Home price
   - Down payment
   - Interest rate
   - Loan term (years)
   - Property tax (annual)
   - Home insurance (annual)
   - PMI (if applicable)
3. View:
   - Monthly payment
   - Total interest paid
   - Amortization schedule

### Tips

- Include property tax and insurance for true monthly cost
- PMI typically required if down payment < 20%
- Compare different scenarios to find affordable options
